Oatmeal

Description

Hello readers,

This is a recipe for oatmeal and it's a quick easy meal for the mornings.

Ingredients

  • 1 oatmeal packet
  • and the will to face any adversity!

Steps

  1. Empty packets into a bowl
  2. Fill packet with water halfway and empty that into the same bowl
  3. Microwave the bowl for 1 minutes. Toodles. :)
